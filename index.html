Vendor onboarding

Host your services inside the Yarra vendor marketplace.

Yarra Consortium connects verified school vendors with member schools through a curated procurement environment. Vendors can publish approved listings, promote seasonal offers, and receive enquiries from schools looking for trusted partners.

About Yarra Consortium

A school collaboration and procurement network.

The platform brings schools, educators, students, programs, content, and vetted vendors into one member-only ecosystem. Vendor visibility is marketplace-only unless Super Admin explicitly grants access to campaign or sponsored program surfaces.

Vendor procedure

  1. Submit company details, category, contact, logo, and consortium offer.
  2. Akshar Arbol/Yarra admin reviews the listing and verifies fit for schools.
  3. Approved vendors go live in the directory with enquiry routing enabled.
  4. Promotional banners and featured placements are submitted for Super Admin approval.
  5. Campaign performance and enquiries are tracked inside the vendor workspace.
